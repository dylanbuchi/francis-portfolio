Honors

The President's Volunteer Service Award

In recognition for 1st Marines 2nd Battalion Aid Station commitment to strengthen our nation and communities through volunteer service (2018).

Global War on Terrorism (E) Medal

Presented for serving in the War on Terror while deployed on the LHD3 ship. This Air-Ground Task Force (MAGTF) were expeditionary quick reaction operations, and response to crisis by our Fleet Marine Force (FMF).

Operations centralized around the 5th and 6th Fleet waterfront and shores, to include Kuwait, Jordan, Oman, *Iran's Strait of Hormuz, Bahrain, Greece and Spain (2018 - 2019).

Navy & Marine Corps Achievement Medal

Consistently demonstrating unrivaled initiative and professional acumen.  Recognizing a manning shortfall within the intensive care unit (ICU), Brempong volunteered and added post-anesthesia care to his skills tool box.

Demonstrated his proficiency by triaging, evaluating, and providing care to ward admissions, surgical recoveries, and conscious sedations contributing to the overall health of the USS KEARSARGE crew, 22 MEU attachments and PHIBRON staff. Never wasted a second throughout the evacuation chain (2018 - 2019).
